Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave, locally known as Guchhupani, is a popular tourist spot in Dehradun. It is famous for its naturally formed caves and a stream of water flowing through them. The refreshing environment and natural beauty make it a favorite picnic spot.

Mindrolling Monastery

Mindrolling Monastery is one of the largest Buddhist centers in India. The monastery complex includes a Great Stupa, a shrine, and beautiful gardens. Visitors can experience the serenity of the place and explore Tibetan Buddhist culture.

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara, meaning 'Thousand-fold Spring', is a scenic waterfall located on the outskirts of Dehradun. The water here is rich in sulfur and is believed to have medicinal properties. The lush green surroundings add to the charm of the place.

Forest Research Institute

The Forest Research Institute (FRI) is a premier institution for forestry research and education. The colonial architecture of the institute is impressive, and the Botanical Garden within the campus is a delight for nature lovers.
